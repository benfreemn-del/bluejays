### Signature moves
- Validation-of-their-work first (rule from Outreach Email Template
  Rules — psych stack #2)
- Specific time-invested ("a few hours this week putting together")
  — rule of thumb #3 in CLAUDE.md
- Humility + implicit gap ("no idea if it's what you had in mind,
  but figured you'd want to see it") — psych stack #4
- Soft reply prompt ("curious what you'd change") — psych stack #5
- Sign-off: just `— Ben` then the email on the next line. Never
  titles. Never company name.
