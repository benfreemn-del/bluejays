# Tweets — Ben's Voice Corpus

Highest-engagement tweets/X posts Ben has personally written. Style
fingerprint, not topic catalog. Target: 50-100 tweets.

Primary fuel for any short-form social-content generator AI features
(future). Also feeds the audit-page hero copy generator + the
follow-up-email punchline generator. The cadence + how Ben opens a
tweet + how he ends one + his question/statement rhythm is the
signal here.

## Status

🟡 **Awaiting content.** Ben to export his top tweets and paste here.

**How to export:**
1. On X (Twitter): Settings → Privacy → "Download an archive of your
   data." One-button export. Comes as a zip.
2. Open `tweets.js` from the export. Sort by `favorite_count +
   retweet_count` descending. Take the top 100.
3. Strip retweets + replies; keep ONLY original tweets.
4. Paste in a flat list — one tweet per blank-line-separated block.

**No frontmatter needed for tweets** (high volume, low per-entry
metadata value). Just paste them in a flat list:

```
[tweet 1]

[tweet 2]

[tweet 3]
```

5. Maintain the order roughly by recency so style drift over time is
   visible.
