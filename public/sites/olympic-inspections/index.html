What We Test For

Today, we are mold experts. The team and the lab are ready as we expand into the rest of the indoor environmental tests homeowners actually need.

🔬

Mold Inspection

Visual walk-through of every floor, attic, crawlspace, and HVAC system. We identify visible growth, moisture patterns, and high-risk areas before sampling.

💨

Air Quality Sampling

Calibrated air pump pulls a measured volume into a spore trap. Lab counts spores by genus and compares indoor vs outdoor baseline.

🧪

Surface & Swab Sampling

Tape-lift or swab samples from suspected growth, sent to an accredited lab. Confirms exactly which species you are dealing with.

📄

Written Lab Reports

Photographic documentation, lab counts side-by-side, plain-English findings, and clear recommendations. Ready for insurance, real estate, or remediation contractors.
