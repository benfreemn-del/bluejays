Tests & Inspections We Offer

If you need it, we’ve probably done it. Wondering if mold is affecting your air quality? Here’s how easy it is to get answers with Olympic Inspections & Testing.

🔬

Complete Mold Inspection

We walk through your space using non-invasive techniques to thoroughly inspect every area for signs of mold and water intrusion. Using thermal imaging, we scan the entire environment to detect hidden moisture, collect key data such as humidity levels, and identify areas of concern that may require sampling. A full inspection includes a comprehensive report detailing our findings along with recommendations for any necessary sampling.

💨

DIY Microbial Air & Surface Sampling

This service is designed for clients who already have a good understanding of what they need and simply want us to handle the sampling process. It’s ideal for those who want to verify whether an issue exists, as well as for contractors working with clients who need confirmation that the air and surfaces are clear. We take care of the sampling so you can focus on the rest.

🧪

ERMI Testing

We offer ERMI testing for clients who are working with healthcare professionals and need to document or track a history of mold exposure. This option is designed to be affordable, accessible, and straightforward, making it easier for clients to get the answers they need.

💧

Water Testing

We test wells and general water quality for peace of mind. Whether it’s a private well, a city tap that suddenly tastes off, or pre-purchase due-diligence, we collect samples and ship them to an accredited lab — coliform bacteria, E. coli, nitrates, lead, arsenic, hardness, pH, and the panel your situation calls for. Real-estate-ready chain-of-custody, plain-English results.

☢️

Radon Testing

Radon is a natural gas that comes up out of the ground. You can’t see it or smell it, and breathing it in over time is the second-biggest cause of lung cancer after smoking. The Olympic Peninsula has higher-than-average levels, especially in older homes and crawlspaces. We test to Washington state standards so you know what’s in your air.

🚽

Septic Inspections

Inspection-only — we don’t pump, repair, or design systems. Washington State requires a septic inspection every 2 years on most permitted systems, and lenders require one for any real-estate transaction on a private system. We handle the inspection and the written report. Only available in certain counties.

About Olympic Inspections & Testing

Family-owned and operated, Olympic Inspections & Testing was established in 2025 to fill a critical gap in high-quality air and mold testing services on the Olympic Peninsula. We encountered few service providers, sky-high prices, and far too many unanswered calls.

That experience inspired us to create something better.
