What Is Reiki

Ancient Healing
Modern Wellness

Reiki is a Japanese energy healing technique that promotes relaxation, reduces stress, and supports your body's natural healing processes. The word Reiki comes from two Japanese words — Rei (universal) and Ki (life energy).

During a session, gentle touch or hands held slightly above the body channels healing energy through specific points. Clients remain fully clothed and typically feel deep relaxation, warmth, or tingling sensations.

Reiki works beautifully alongside conventional medicine and other holistic therapies, making it a perfect complement to your existing wellness routine.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

No belief is required. Reiki works with your body's natural energy regardless of your belief system. Many clients come in curious or even skeptical and still experience noticeable shifts. An open mind is helpful, but it is not a requirement.

Most clients feel warmth, tingling, or a pleasant heaviness in the body. Many fall into a deeply relaxed, meditative state. Some experience vivid imagery or emotional release. Every session is unique — there is no "right" way to experience Reiki.

Reiki is completely non-invasive and safe for people of all ages, including children and those undergoing medical treatment. After a session some clients feel temporarily tired as the body integrates the healing — this usually passes within a day. Drinking extra water after your session is recommended.

A single session can bring noticeable relief. For deeper, lasting shifts, most practitioners recommend a series of 3–4 sessions close together, then monthly maintenance. PRACTITIONER will discuss a plan that fits your specific needs and goals during your first visit.

Wear comfortable, loose clothing — you remain fully clothed throughout the session. Leave jewelry at home if possible, and avoid heavy meals directly beforehand. Bring any intentions, questions, or areas of focus you'd like to work on — PRACTITIONER will incorporate them into your session.

Reiki energy is not limited by physical proximity. During a distance session, you simply find a comfortable, quiet place at the scheduled time. PRACTITIONER transmits Reiki across space using the Hon Sha Ze Sho Nen symbol, and clients routinely report experiencing the same sensations they feel in person.

Ready to Begin Your Healing Journey?

Your first session is the most important step. PRACTITIONER welcomes clients of all backgrounds — whether you are navigating stress, seeking balance, or simply curious about what Reiki can offer.
