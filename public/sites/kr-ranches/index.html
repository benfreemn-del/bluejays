Who We Are

Grown Here.
Sold Direct.

KR Ranches is a family operation in Prosser, Washington — right in the Yakima Valley, where the soil is rich and the seasons are real. We raise beef, pork, lamb, and chicken the way it's supposed to be done: on pasture, without shortcuts, and without the industrial supply chain.

When you buy from us, you're not buying from a warehouse or a grocery distributor. You're buying from the people who fed, raised, and cared for every animal on this list. That's a different relationship with your food — and it tastes like it.

The Difference

Why Ranch-Direct Matters

You Know the Source

No mystery. No supply chain guesswork. The animal on your plate lived on our Prosser ranch — and we can tell you exactly how.

No Hormones. No Antibiotics.

We never use added hormones or routine antibiotics. Our animals are healthy because they're raised right, not because they're medicated.

Better Price Per Pound

When you buy direct and in bulk, you cut out every middleman margin. Custom orders in particular deliver exceptional value for quality that grocery stores can't touch.

Supports a Local Family

Every dollar goes directly to a Washington family, not a national corporation. Buying from us keeps family ranching alive in the Yakima Valley.

It Just Tastes Better

Pasture-raised, properly finished meat has a flavor and texture that you simply cannot get from a feedlot animal. Taste it once and the grocery aisle feels different.

Washington Raised

We're proud to be part of the Prosser community and the broader WA agricultural tradition. When you eat KR Ranches, you eat local — for real.
