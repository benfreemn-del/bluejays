Donate to LCAC

Every dollar stays in Lewis County, supporting autistic individuals, neurodiverse families, and the community programs they rely on.

Where Your Gift Goes

Your Donation in Action

100% of donations directly fund Coalition programs — sensory-friendly events, family support groups, the Spectrum & Development Center, and respite services for rural families.

$25

Sensory supplies for one family receiving services

$50

One caregiver attends a quarterly support group with materials

$100

A family receives a full year of resource navigation

$250

Two kids attend a week of summer sensory programming

Tax-Deductible Giving

Lewis County Autism Coalition is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Donations are tax-deductible to the fullest extent allowed by law. You'll receive a receipt for your records by mail or email within two weeks of your gift.
