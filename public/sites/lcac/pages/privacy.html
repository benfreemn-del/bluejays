Privacy Policy

How the Lewis County Autism Coalition handles the information you share with us. Last updated: May 2026.

Who we are

Lewis County Autism Coalition ("LCAC," "we," "us") is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit based in Napavine, Washington. This policy explains what we collect on lcautism.org and how we use it.

Information we collect

When you fill out one of our forms (Contact, Volunteer, Sponsor, Newsletter, or Event RSVP), we collect:

  • Your name and email address
  • Optional information you provide (phone, message text, volunteer interests, sponsorship preferences)

If you accept analytics cookies, we may also collect anonymized usage data (pages viewed, time on site, general location). You can decline this in the cookie banner.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiry or request
  • To send you updates about Coalition events, programs, and opportunities (only if you signed up for them)
  • To coordinate volunteer placements and event RSVPs
  • To process and acknowledge donations and sponsorships
  • To improve our programs and the website

Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. We share it only with: (a) Coalition staff and volunteers who need it to respond to you; (b) trusted service providers (email delivery, form processing) who help us run the website — bound by confidentiality; and (c) authorities if required by law.

Email opt-out

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email info@lcautism.org any time to remove yourself from our list.

Cookies & analytics

The website uses essential cookies to function and (with your consent) Google Analytics 4 to understand traffic patterns. Analytics is denied by default until you opt in via the cookie banner. We anonymize IP addresses and never use analytics for advertising.

Children's privacy

Our programs serve children and families, but the website is intended for adults (parents, caregivers, supporters). We do not knowingly collect information directly from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights

You can request a copy of the information we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to delete it. Email info@lcautism.org.

Questions

Email info@lcautism.org or write to us at 1673 S. Market St. PMB #240, Chehalis, WA 98532.
