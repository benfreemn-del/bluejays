Signs You May Have a Mold Problem

Don't wait until you're sick.

Musty or Earthy Odor

A persistent damp, musty smell is one of the most reliable early indicators of hidden mold growth.

Visible Dark Spots

Black, green, or grey spots on walls, ceilings, or grout that return after cleaning need professional assessment.

Recent Water Damage

Flooding, leaks, or burst pipes create ideal conditions for mold within 24–48 hours if not properly dried.

Allergy Symptoms at Home

Sneezing, coughing, or congestion that improves when you leave may signal elevated indoor spore counts.

Condensation on Surfaces

Persistent moisture on windows, walls, or pipes indicates humidity levels where mold thrives.

Roof Leak or Plumbing Issue

Any past or ongoing leak, even one that seems resolved, warrants a mold inspection to clear the structure.
