# Independent Contractor Agreement

**Between:** BlueJay Business Solutions LLC ("Company", "BlueJays")
**And:** [Madie's full legal name] ("Contractor")
**Effective date:** [DATE — fill before signing]

---

## 1. Engagement

Company engages Contractor as an **independent sales contractor** to set
appointments and refer prospective customers to BlueJays for the sale of:

- (a) **Custom website services** (the "Website Product"), priced at
  approximately $997 + $100/year maintenance.
- (b) **AI Marketing System / backend buildouts** (the "AI System
  Product"), priced at approximately $9,700 one-time.

Contractor's role is **appointment-setting only**. Contractor does NOT
have authority to bind BlueJays to any agreement, modify pricing, accept
payment, or close sales on behalf of BlueJays. The closing of every
sale, the issuing of every contract, and the collection of every payment
remain BlueJays' sole responsibility.

## 2. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is engaged as an **independent contractor**, NOT an employee,
partner, joint venturer, or agent of BlueJays.

- Contractor is solely responsible for all federal, state, local, and
  self-employment taxes on payments received.
- Contractor is **not entitled** to employee benefits including but not
  limited to health insurance, paid leave, retirement contributions,
  workers' compensation, or unemployment insurance.
- Contractor sets her own hours and methods, subject to compliance with
  the BlueJays sales script and brand standards (Section 6).
- Contractor will receive an IRS Form 1099-NEC by January 31 of each
  following year if total compensation in the prior calendar year
  exceeds $600.

## 3. Compensation

Contractor is paid **commission only**. No base salary, draw, retainer,
or hourly wage.

| Closed sale type | Madie's commission |
|---|---|
| Website Product close ($997) | **$200** per closed sale |
| AI System Product close ($9,700) | **$1,000** per closed sale |

A "closed sale" means: the prospect signs the BlueJays Service Agreement
**and** the corresponding payment (full payment OR the first installment
of a multi-pay plan) clears BlueJays' bank account.

Commissions are paid via Venmo, Zelle, or direct deposit to an account
designated by Contractor, on the regular Friday following the close.

If a customer requests a refund or chargeback within the agreed
service-guarantee window, **the related commission is reversed** and
deducted from the Contractor's next payout.

## 4. Term and Termination

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues until
terminated by either party.

- **Either party may terminate with 7 days' written notice** for any
  reason (email is sufficient).
- BlueJays may terminate **immediately** for cause, defined as:
  (a) misrepresentation of BlueJays products or pricing,
  (b) violation of customer-confidentiality obligations,
  (c) any unlawful conduct,
  (d) damage to BlueJays' reputation, or
  (e) material breach of this Agreement.
- On termination, commissions earned on previously-closed sales remain
  payable on the normal payout schedule. No commissions accrue on
  prospects pitched but not yet closed at the termination date.

## 5. Confidentiality

Contractor will encounter BlueJays customer data, prospect lists,
pricing strategy, scripts, and internal tools. Contractor agrees:

- All BlueJays prospect lists, customer information, internal scripts,
  call recordings, dashboards, and pricing details are confidential.
- Contractor will use this information **only** in the performance of
  her duties under this Agreement.
- Contractor will not disclose any of the above to third parties,
  including any current or future employer, business partner, or
  competitor of BlueJays.
- This obligation survives termination of this Agreement indefinitely
  for trade secrets, and for **2 years** for other confidential
  information.

On termination, Contractor will return or delete all BlueJays materials
in her possession (digital and physical) within 7 days.

## 6. Brand Standards and Compliance

Contractor will:

- Represent BlueJays accurately. Use the BlueJays scripts, talking
  points, and pricing as published. Do not invent product features,
  guarantees, or pricing not approved by BlueJays.
- Comply with all applicable telemarketing laws including the federal
  Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and the Do-Not-Call Registry.
  Honor every "remove me from your list" request immediately.
- Identify herself as Madie, an independent contractor working on
  behalf of BlueJays. Do not represent yourself as an employee or owner
  of BlueJays.

## 7. Non-Solicitation

During the term of this Agreement and for **12 months** after
termination, Contractor will not:

- Solicit BlueJays customers for any competing service.
- Recruit BlueJays' customers, partners, or contractors for any
  competing business.
- Use BlueJays prospect lists or call data to start or support any
  competing sales effort.

This does NOT prevent Contractor from working in the broader sales
industry — only from using BlueJays-derived information or relationships.

## 8. Indemnification

Contractor will indemnify and hold BlueJays harmless from any claim,
loss, or liability arising out of:

- Contractor's misrepresentation of BlueJays products or services.
- Contractor's violation of telemarketing laws or this Agreement.
- Contractor's negligence or willful misconduct.

## 9. Dispute Resolution

Any dispute arising under this Agreement will be resolved first by
good-faith negotiation between the parties. If unresolved within 30
days, the dispute will be submitted to binding arbitration in
**[Snohomish County, Washington]** under the rules of the American
Arbitration Association. The losing party bears the arbitration costs.

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Washington,
without regard to its conflict of laws principles.

## 10. Entire Agreement

This Agreement is the **entire understanding** between the parties
regarding the subject matter. It supersedes all prior discussions,
emails, and verbal agreements. Modifications must be in writing,
signed by both parties.

If any provision is held unenforceable, the remainder of this
Agreement remains in effect.
