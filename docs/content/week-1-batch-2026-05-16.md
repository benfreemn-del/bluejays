## The batch (5 scripts, 30 seconds each)

Each follows the Hormozi 4-beat template from the content-engine skill:
HOOK (0-3s) → PROMISE (3-8s) → PROOF (8-22s) → CTA (22-30s).

Each uses ONE of the 6 hook structures. No two clips share a hook
structure or content bucket — that's the kaleidoscope rule.
